Growth Forecasting Model

Our financial projections utilize advanced CAGR modeling for accurate future earnings estimates

Compound Annual Growth Rate

$$ CAGR = \left( \frac{\text{Ending Value}}{\text{Beginning Value}} \right)^{\frac{1}{\text{n}}} - 1 $$
Ending Value (EV)
Investment value at period end
Beginning Value (BV)
Investment value at period start
n (periods)
Number of compounding years
